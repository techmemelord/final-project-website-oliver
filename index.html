Welcome to the website about soccer

What is soccer?

Soccer is a sport that is played worldwide by billions of people around the world in many different countries and places. This sport is a universal one in the fact that anyone of any physicality can play.

What are the basics of this sport?

This sport is highly simple to learn, but hard to master. There are multiple skills you can learn for this sport that correspond to different positions such as shooting (how you score goals), dribbling, defending, and goalkeeping (how well you can save the ball and prevent it from entering into your net).

But if you want it in the most basic way possible, do you want it in just 10 words or less? In that case, you basically kick a ball into a net

What benefits may this sport give to the person who plays it?

There are multiple ways the sport benefits the player, one of these being health benefits. This is because you are exercising and using your energy and muscles to play, which will contribute to a healthier life in the long term such as lower risks of heart attacks. It could also help them financially as professional athletes who play this sport get paid to play which could be for a team. In a nutshell, you get a salary for playing professionally on a team.

What physical attributes do you need for certain position in the sport?

While the game is universal, there certain player positions that may rely on physicality in order for the person who plays there to be good.

The first one which may be notable is goalkeepers in which where you have to be tall to be good. If you aren't tall, well, you can still be good, but you will likely concede more goals, typically from chip shots and aerial battles.

Most posititons do not require specific physical attributes in order to be good at that position. But some physical attributes are important to be good, but there aren't many.
