Defensive Positions

These positions mainly help stop the ball from entering the net

Goalkeeper (GK)

The goalkeeper is the player that by all means necessary, wants to stop the ball from entering their net. They have the most unique sets of skills on the pitch in that they can use their hands within their penalty box. With their ability to use their hands, they can save, catch, and punch the ball out to prevent a dangerous chance from going in.
